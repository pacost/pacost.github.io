ABOUT US

Our passion is to support outstanding entrepreneurs in the growth and expansion of their companies.

Following a “real value added” philosophy, we work hand in hand with our portfolio companies in all aspects of their Mexico operations, and their global strategies.

With the support of distinguished institutional and private investors, at XB Ventures we seek to become the trusted local partner of innovative companies which have the potential to become leaders in their respective industries.

Management Team

Hernando Guzmán

Hernando is a passionate entrepreneur and investor. He currently serves as a managing partner and is a co-founder of XB Ventures, and is also an angel investor. Hernando has ample experience as an entrepreneur, he has founded several companies, has worked in VC funds and in global consulting companies. Hernando holds an MBA from The Johnson School at Cornell University and a Bachelors degree in Mechanical and Electrical Engineering from Universidad Iberoamericana. He is also a member of The Society of Kauffman Fellows. Hernando is an avid marathon runner and an enthusiastic fantasy novel reader.

Alberto Tubilla

Alberto is a devote investor in startups. He currently serves as a managing partner and is a co-founder of XB Ventures, and is also an angel investor. Alberto’s ample experience comes from his work in VC funds, technology, ecommerce and telecom companies, and as an entrepreneur. He is also an investor in the hospitality industry. Alberto holds an MBA from The F.W. Olin Graduate School of Business at Babson College, an MSC from the King’s College London and a Bachelors degree in Electronic and Telecommunications Engineering from Universidad Iberoamericana. Alberto was the First Latin American to be accepted into The Society of Kauffman Fellows.
